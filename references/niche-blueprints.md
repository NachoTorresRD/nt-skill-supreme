# NT-Design Engine: Niche Architectural Blueprints

*High-converting site structures, color palettes, font pairings, and section cadence by industry.*

---

## 🏥 1. Medical, Dental & Healthcare Clinics
- **Primary Goal**: Instant trust, online appointment booking, clear service pricing.
- **Palette**: Clean Slate White/Navy (`#0f172a`), Medical Cyan (`#06b6d4`), Soft Mint (`#10b981`).
- **Fonts**: *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Body) + *Outfit* (Headings).
- **Mandatory Cadence**:
  1. Hero with Emergency/Online Booking CTA + Doctor Social Proof Stack.
  2. Floating Quick Stats (Patients Treated, Years of Experience, Rating 4.9/5).
  3. Services Grid (Dental Implants, Orthodontics, Emergency Care, Cosmetic).
  4. Interactive Doctor Bio / Team Carousel.
  5. Before/After Results Showcase or Client Video Testimonials.
  6. Direct Online Booking Form + Map & Working Hours.
  7. Floating WhatsApp Button ("Book Consultation on WhatsApp").

## 🏋️ 2. Fitness, Gyms & Personal Trainers
- **Primary Goal**: High energy, free trial / day pass lead capture, membership sales.
- **Palette**: Pitch Black (`#09090b`), Electric Amber (`#f59e0b`) or Crimson Red (`#ef4444`), Off-White.
- **Fonts**: *Space Grotesk* (Headings) + *Inter* (Body).
- **Mandatory Cadence**:
  1. Hero with High-Energy Background Overlay + "Claim 3-Day Free Pass" CTA.
  2. Gym Highlights Counter (24/7 Access, Personal Trainers, Sauna, Free Parking).
  3. Class Schedule & Specialized Programs.
  4. Interactive Membership Pricing Cards (Monthly vs Annual Toggle).
  5. Member Transformations (Before/After Slider & Testimonials).
  6. Lead Form: "Get Your Free Pass Sent to WhatsApp/Email".

## ⚖️ 3. Professional Services (Law Firms, Accounting, Consulting)
- **Primary Goal**: Authority, confidentiality, free initial consultation booking.
- **Palette**: Deep Royal Navy (`#0b1329`), Gold Accent (`#d97706`), Warm Crisp Surface (`#f8fafc`).
- **Fonts**: *Cinzel* or *Playfair Display* (Display) + *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Body).
- **Mandatory Cadence**:
  1. Hero with Authoritative Heading + "Request Confidential Consultation".
  2. Practice Areas Grid (Corporate Law, Real Estate, Litigation, Tax).
  3. Case Victories / Proven Track Record Counter ($50M+ Won / 98% Success).
  4. Senior Partners & Attorney Profiles.
  5. Consultation Scheduler Form with Date/Time Picker.

## 🍽️ 4. Local Gastronomy, Restaurants & Cafes
- **Primary Goal**: Appetizing visual appeal, online menu preview, table reservation.
- **Palette**: Dark Espresso (`#120c08`), Warm Copper (`#ea580c`), Cream White (`#fef3c7`).
- **Fonts**: *Playfair Display* (Headings) + *Plus Jakarta Sans* (Body).
- **Mandatory Cadence**:
  1. Hero with Full-Screen Food Background + "Reserve Table" / "Order Online" CTAs.
  2. Signature Dishes Carousel / Visual Category Grid (Starters, Mains, Cocktails).
  3. Daily Specials & Chef Recommendations.
  4. Table Reservation Widget (Party Size, Date, Time).
  5. Location, Parking Info, Hours & Direct Google Maps Embed Link.

## 💻 5. Digital Agencies, Freelancers & SaaS Apps
- **Primary Goal**: High-tech polish, portfolio showcase, instant quote estimation.
- **Palette**: Dark Slate Glass (`#07070a`), Vibrant Purple (`#8b5cf6`), Cyan Glow (`#06b6d4`).
- **Fonts**: *Outfit* (Headings) + *Inter* (Body) + *JetBrains Mono* (Code/Metrics).
- **Mandatory Cadence**:
  1. Hero with Animated Ambient Spotlight + "Book Demo" / "Get Free Quote" CTAs.
  2. Client Logo Ticker (Social Proof Bar).
  3. Interactive Feature / Services Bento Grid.
  4. Live Portfolio Case Studies with Metrics (+240% Growth).
  5. Tiered Pricing Matrix.
  6. Accordion FAQ Section.
  7. Conversion Contact Form + Direct Calendar Embed.
