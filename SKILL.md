## 🚀 Core Operational Pillars

### 1. Aesthetic Taste & Zero-Slop Excellence
- **Anti-Slop Protocol**: Prohibit plain white cards with generic blue buttons (`#3b82f6`) or centered 3-card grid templates.
- **Curated HSL Systems**: Define adaptive light/dark color tokens, subtle radial spotlights, ambient glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur-md`), and high-contrast typography.
- **Fluid Font Pairings**: Pair geometric display typefaces (*Outfit*, *Space Grotesk*, *Playfair Display*) with readable body fonts (*Plus Jakarta Sans*, *Inter*) and monospaced badges (*JetBrains Mono*).

### 2. Physical Motion & Tactile Spring Feedback
- **Tactile Click Feedback**: All interactive buttons, cards, and links MUST feature a instant scale press response (`transform: scale(0.96)`) at 100ms.
- **Scroll-Driven GPU Animations**: Utilize native CSS `animation-timeline: view()` and `scroll()` for zero-lag scroll reveals and reading progress indicators.
- **3D Perspective Hover Tilt**: Interactive cards respond to cursor coordinates with 3D rotation (`rotateX`, `rotateY`) and specular glare overlays.

### 3. 2026 CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) Standard
- **The 3-Second Rule**: Hero headlines must instantly clarify *What it is*, *What benefit it delivers*, and *What action to take*.
- **Near-CTA Social Proof**: Anchor star ratings `★★★★★`, client counts, and trust badges directly adjacent to primary call-to-action buttons.
- **Minimalist Friction-Free Forms**: Keep lead forms to 2–3 fields max (Name + WhatsApp/Phone) to yield 100%+ conversion lifts.
- **Ergonomic Mobile Anchors**: Sticky bottom thumb-action bar on mobile devices.

### 4. Dynamic Visual Asset & Graceful Fallback Engine
- **AI Image Generation**: When image generation tools are available, synthesize 16:9 hero photography, 4:3 bento images, and 1:1 team portraits.
- **Multi-Tier Fallbacks**: If image generation is unavailable, fall back gracefully to high-resolution Unsplash photo URLs, gradient SVG icons, or glassmorphic badges. NEVER output raw broken text boxes.
