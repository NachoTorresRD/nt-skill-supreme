## 🌟 What NT-SKILL SUPREME Improved

Without a skill framework, standard AI outputs default to plain white cards with generic `#3b82f6` blue buttons and zero interactive depth. **NT-SKILL SUPREME** enforced:

- **12 Full Sections**: Sticky Navbar, 3-Second Hero, Core Benefits, 3D Tilt Services, 4-Step Process, Smile Simulator, Team Showcase, FAQ Accordion, Booking Form, Floating WhatsApp, Footer, and Showcase Banner.
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)**: 3-Second value rule, single visually dominant primary CTA (`Reservar Evaluación`), near-CTA proof badges, and streamlined 2-field lead form.
- **Physical Motion Physics**: Instant tactile scale shrink `:active scale(0.96)` at 100ms, cursor-following 3D perspective tilt with specular glare (`@media (hover: hover)`), and scroll progress bar.
- **Accessible Standards**: Keyboard focus rings (`:focus-visible`), ARIA landmarks, modal focus management, system reduced motion overrides (`@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)`).
- **Graceful Asset Fallback**: High-resolution Unsplash photo fallbacks with explicit dimensions (`width`, `height`, `loading="lazy"`).
